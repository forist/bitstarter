Based on customer's profile and actual mood, Moodeh recommends activities for enhancing the mood. This service, stores the customer's characteristics collected with a series of psychological and social tests. Also other preferences, likes, dislikes, social activity, are collected too. Actual mood is assessed with a combination of the well established quick tests. Based on profile, historical data, customer's activity and feedback, centrally gathered statistical data the service's offerings improve over a longer period of time.

Primary income is expected from paying customers, targeted ads from the free version and vent of statistical anonymized data. To quickstart the service funds are raised for the development of the central application which allows registration of customers, profile building and completion of mood evaluation tests.
